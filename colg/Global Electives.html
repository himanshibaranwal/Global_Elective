About the system of exercises

The proposed system of exercises in essence, the same gymnastics, but supplemented by personal development and spirituality. The proposed methodic of the development of the voice upon condition systematic use can also be very effective for the treatment of the following diseases:

  • asthma, other chronic diseases of the bronchi and lungs;
  • neuroses, fear, anxiety, fear of the audience;
  • increased fatigue; migraine;
  • reduced immunity and general resistance of body; stuttering;
  • restoration of lost voices of singers etc
